Moroccan Jewish Caravan

Launched in February 2014, the Caravan engages its comrades and the general public to celebrate Judeo-Moroccan culture through a series of events: a campus visit, multimedia exhibitions in the main civic institutions, kosher cooking workshops, performances music and a distribution of books. campaign for schoolchildren. Activities bridge generational and religious divides, promote mutual understanding, and cultivate compassionate peacemakers.
