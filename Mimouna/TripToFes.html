Trip To Fes

On Sunday 25th 2018, the Mimouna Club AUI visited several Moroccan-Jewish sites including the cemetery and three synagogues in Fes. This trip was the opportunity to know more about the Moroccan-Jewish way of life and discover many elements of the Jewish Culture. We had the pleasure to meet Mr. Abraham Sebbagh, rabbi of Fes, as well as Mr Elie Devico, treasurer of the Jewish community of Fes. We hope that the ones who attended the event enjoyed !!
