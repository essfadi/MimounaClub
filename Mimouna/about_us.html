what IS MIMOUNA?

Mimouna Club is one of the outstanding clubs of Al Akhawayn University in Ifrane. Located in the heart of AUI campus, Mimouna Club (first called Moadon Mimouna) was founded in April, 2007. With 300 members, this club hosted several activities and distinguished guests. With great leadership skills, passion and dedication to the Moroccan Jewish culture, this club carries the message of Mimouna Association, which is promoting and preserving the Moroccan-Jewish traditions and heritage.

The name «Mimouna» symbolizes a common heritage of Moroccans. Traditionally celebrated at the end of Passover, Mimouna is a traditional Jewish Moroccan celebration. Jewish families often invite their Muslim neighbors Muslims who, among other things, bring bread to join the festivities.
MIMOUNA seeks to educate Moroccans about the Jewish culture that flourished in the country, and encourage harmony between Jews and Muslims.
