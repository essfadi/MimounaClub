ABOUT US

MIMOUNA CLUB

A cultural non-profit association created in 2007 by young Muslim students willing to promote and preserve the Jewish-Moroccan heritage.

The Association has since expanded these activities on a national level in order to engage in the education of the Moroccan youth about the Moroccan Jewish heritage. This awareness is particularly achieved through the «Jewish-Moroccan heritage Days» and through educational activities designed for high school and graduate students.

One of the founding principles of the Association is to reclaim the cultural diversity of Morocco through its history. In a context where intolerance and extremism are rampant, Mimouna, as a Citizen Patriot Association, would like to contribute to the preserving and strengthening of the plural Moroccan identity.

This department is all about organization. During events, they play a crucial role in ensuring that everything goes accordingly and runs smoothly through their guidance and planning methods. 8 members have devoted their time for this task.

The IT department oversees the installation and maintenance of our website. Its main function is to ensure that the network runs smoothly. The IT department must ensure the smooth functionality of the website, and ensure that our page visitors are up to date on Mimouna´s latest prospects.
